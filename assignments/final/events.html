Cycle the Continental Divide

This annual event brings together some of the best bikers in the world. It's an opportunity to give money to charity while cycling the beautiful north to south trail.The ride has spectacular terrain and scenery. The entire route is dirt-road and mountain-pass riding every day.

Start Date: October 8, 2018
End Date: November 8, 2018
Trail Location: Banff, Alberta Canada to Antelope Wells, New Mexico
Skill Level: Advanced
Price: $525.00
